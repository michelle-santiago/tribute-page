ABOUT

“Every action you take is a vote for the type of person you wish to become.” ― James Clear, Atomic Habits: An Easy & Proven Way to Build Good Habits & Break Bad Ones

James Clear is an author, speaker, entrepreneur and photographer. His works focused on habits, decision-making and continuous improvement. He wrote Atomic Habits, which has sold more than 8 million copies worldwide. It has been on the New York Times bestseller list for over one year. He lives with his wife in Columbus, Ohio, United States. Click here to visit his website

ATOMIC HABITS

How and Why James Wrote It

In November 2012, he began publishing articles at jamesclear.com about his personal experiments with habits. Within a few months, his email subscribers had grown to one thousand and by the end of 2013 that number had grown to more than thirty thousand people. In 2015, he reached two hundred thousand email subscribers and signed a book deal with Penguin Random House to begin writing Atomic Habits.
